TacoCat: A Coding Project

TacoCat is a program to determine if the word or phrase entered is a palindrome. A palindrome is a word or phrase that reads the same backward as forward.

Examples of palindrome

tacocat

racecar

civic

radar

Instructions

Enter a word or phrase. All white spaces and special characters will be removed.
